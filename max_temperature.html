Max Temperature

In this visualization, temperatures for cities near and at the equator (latitude 0) are higher than temperatures of cities that are more near the poles specifically cities in the Northern Hemisphere. This is expected since the Northern Hemisphere is still tilted a little bit away from the sun at this time of year.
