Summary: Latitude vs. X

This assignment was to explore different weather changes and patterns as one is expected experience when approaching closer to the equator. Using citipy library and OpenWeather API, four scatter plots were created to visualize four weather factors against latitude: maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.

The four visualizations are displayed on the right of this page. Visitors can click on any of the visualizations and be directed to the respective page for a more in-depth explanation. Comparison between the four visualizations and additional data are provided in the top right links. Enjoy and happy data-ing!
